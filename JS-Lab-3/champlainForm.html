Step 1 - Identification



Note : If you have previously studied in Québec, you will find your Permanent Code on any transcript or report card issued by your school. It is made up of 4 letters and 8 digits. IT IS NOT YOUR HEALTH INSURANCE NUMBER. Leave it blank if you have never studied in Québec.



The validation of the permanent code uses the given information in the form. Please, validate that your 'Family name at birth','First name','Sex' and 'Date of birth' are correctly filled.

Note : If you do not have a Permanent Code, enter below any other first name(s) that appear on your birth certificate and that you did not enter above or check the box stating that you have none.
